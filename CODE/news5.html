Squad Boost + End of Year Event Pass—Live Now

A Squad Boost is active, just in time for the end of year Event Pass.

Community | Kenny “Karnifexlol“ Cameron | 4/22/2025

No, not that kind of boosted. Squad boosted. From now until 2:00PM PST on January 7, 2025, when you party up with your squad, you’ll rack up extra XP and more progress toward your rewards. Here’s how the XP scaling works: Party of 1 - 0% increase Party of 2 - 8% increase Party of 3 - 12% increase Party of 4 - 16% increase Party of 5 - 20% increase The more the merrier, right?
